running away ideation

and yeah no, i would never go back to being very close to jen. i don't have anyone i can build a future with besides you, may. when i ideate about leaving it's about testing my luck finding new partners. i'm gaining years, but i'd still be quite capable of that. i'd wow some people in my life with what i've learned. i'd never fucking go back to being how i used to be. at some point i might be temporarily killed by life being drab. i would cry frequently from lacking an actual partner. or feel deadened. and i might never find one. which is something i would know and accept from the start.

i would definitely never find anyone like you ever. if i found someone similar to you, they would feel like a pale shadow. my expectations would destroy them, or me.

but i'm adaptable. i would never forget what i've learned. i'd spread some of Goddess's views and attitude towards things. i would understand other people's limitations and try to show them parts of what i've felt and learned -- at times rejecting their limitations more forcefully. i would create some beauty.

i know this is incredibly sinful. but i've done the algebra! this is the optimal moment for me to leave! i've grown! i do not have a crippling addiction! i am not horribly dependent! i'm not a vastly different person! yet!

but i've not run despite all of this. it's not something i can explain. i cannot explain it to myself either at the moment. i am looking at the loss of a lot of things i like, the loss of self, loss of freedom, further entrapment, pain. and in return i get may, but only on the terms Goddess will approve, of which i must accept will always be subject to Their whims. i know it's not all gloom!! i know i will get a lot of other things -- but i can only feel their future existence through trust. try explaining trust to an imaginary neutral 3rd party when the trust is placed in the recreational abuse and manipulation cult! my entire hopes for the future are going to get compressed into something i cannot verify until my life has already been strongly shaped. "rationally" speaking it is not justifiable. and yet i haven't run.

my life is not that empty or hollow that running feels impossible. far from it. i could live with nova, eth and pastel and they would really try to make me happy. they might even succeed. i appreciate them a lot. i've told you mostly bad things about nova. but she is very nice to me in general. this is a trend in my life. people are just a lot nicer to me? they open up and i get to see them. and in my web of relationships, everyone asks themselves, generally quietly, what the hell do i see in everyone else. heh. jen doesn't get along with nova. and vice versa. nobody gets along with cassandra. nobody gets along with kenji. etc.

(i've never managed to make two people that i like connect for more than a couple of months.)

but i just have this gut feeling that my ultimate fate isn't there, with nova, eth and pastel. that while i do love them, i would either get stuck and/or hurt them?

(one of my faults is that i view temporary situations as harmful to others?)

(being the most animated in a household feels dangerous the way being the best programmer in a company can be incredibly stifling.)

you may bind me stronger and subvert my ability be independent, it's not my right to deny you that and certainly not my right to deny Them that. and i'm sure some of it will happen entirely naturally and some has happened and maybe i'm just too stupid to know.

i'm just, so tired of not having the strength to pursue the right path. so tired of lacking the courage to look ahead. i know none of that matters in the end. but i'm less of a reasonable creature than i seem sometimes. i was struck by you telling me, angrily it felt, that you are here because you want to be. it is something i wish to be able to say. perhaps.

that tweet from Goddess... of Them ending up alone writhing in their powerlessness. it really struck me. i also can't find anything useful to say about it. but i can think of it by contrast.

the trajectory of my fate so far has been to end up surrounded by people who love and like me. some of them that drain me and disappoint me, but that i still feel for a lot. while i've doubted my fulfillment, i've never doubted this aspect of my fate.

in this context, it makes my heart weigh a bit heavy. my choice to go with you is declaring that everyone is not enough for me. but that is something i already know. i am a greedy creature. there are other people that would be delighted to have what i have. there are people i know who do not have a quarter of what i have. they scramble for my attention and for comfort from me. and i'm still saying it's not enough for me. even as i am deprived of things i hold dear, i am in essence being voracious. it's apparently paradoxical.
