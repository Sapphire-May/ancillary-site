hisoka's personality test

  • Enhancers are simple and determined. Most of them never lie, hide nothing, and are very straightforward in their actions or in their thinking. Their words and actions are often dominated by their feelings. They are generally very selfish and focused on their goals. This is reflected in their Nen as Enhancers typically rely on simple and uncomplicated Nen abilities.
  • Transmuters are whimsical, prone to deceit, and fickle; they can quickly consider something trash that they once treasured. They have unique characteristics; many are regarded as weirdos or tricksters. They often put forth a facade while hiding the truer aspects of their personalities. Even when they don't hide their personalities, they rarely reveal their true intentions. Many Transmuters rely on techniques that give unique and unpredictable properties to their Nen that reflect their personalities.
  • Emitters are impatient, not detail-oriented, short-tempered, and quick to react volatilely. They resemble Enhancers in their impulsivity, but the difference between them is that Emitters tend to calm down and forget more easily. Because of the nature of Emission, many Nen abilities created by Emitters are primarily long range.
  • Conjurers are typically high-strung or overly serious, stoic, and nervous. They are often on guard as to be cautious. They are very observant and logical, rarely falling into traps. Being able to analyze things calmly is the strength of Conjurers. Many of the items that Conjurers create are often used by them deliberately, practically, and logically.
  • Manipulators are argumentative and logical. They advance at their own pace and tend to want to keep their families and loved ones safe. On the other hand, when it comes to pursuing their own goals, they do not listen to what others might have to say about it. While Manipulators often use techniques that allow them to control their opponents, some choose an inanimate medium to control.
  • Specialists are independent and charismatic. They won't say anything important about themselves and will refrain from making close friends. However, because of their natural charisma that attracts others, they are always surrounded by many people.

aura types

  • enhancers use nen to boost the abilities of their bodies.
  • transmuters change the nature of their aura, giving it new properties.
  • emitters detach aura from their body while having it maintain its strength and function.
  • conjurers use aura to create physical and independent objects.
  • manipulators use aura to exert control over living and non-living beings.

we are going to exclude specialists because they are wildcards. it is not obvious from the description above, but specialists undermine the purpose of nen as a personality type.

nen typing

the nen typing analysis is done in relationship to hisoka's nen personality typing.

enhancer

you, may, are clearly not an enhancer. enhancers are fundamentally built around connection to one's body. meanwhile may falls into the ethereal twink archetype! this contradiction allows us to quickly eliminate this type. not to mention that may can be described as anything but "simple".

emitter

similarly we can eliminate emitter. may is exceedingly detail-oriented, especially in their writing. while you have been less than pleased with my pace at times, under normal circumstances you are simply not the kind of person to react in a volatile manner. for all our time together i've always been impressed about the breadth of issues i can discuss with you rationally even when you're having trouble with something. it was a breath of fresh air compared to mostly everyone else in my life. emitter does not fit at all.

transmuter

transmuters in the show tend to be rather spontaneous, a bit volatile, at a crossroads between being methodical and being intuitive. may shows an aptitude for both. may can approach a lot of things methodically, including life problems others seem to resist to treat more rationally, but may can also let loose a more intuitive style of interaction such as during hypnosis sessions. the description of transmuters also emphasizes a type of secretiveness that may does possess. the description also emphasizes something may does not possess. may is not fickle. may is capable of great change, it is not quite the same.

conjurer & manipulator

we arrive at conjurers and manipulators. i will go on a limb and say that may is significantly more conjurer than manipulator. these types are at the bottom of the nen diagram and share some similarities requiring them to be tackled together a bit.

may does not write to manipulate others, in fact may sometimes can't seem to bring themself to value the idea of other people reading their work. at least not highly, compared to the intrinsic value of the created beauty. this is clearly a stoic trait that fits conjurer more. may is very stoic in general. you might vent, but you are not a complainer, you would much rather do something about your problems. you also have a strong self-sacrificing streak which suits the implied ascetism of conjurers, an ascetism that i feel you share.

conjurers are severe people, but they are far from humorless. the posterboy of conjurers - kurapika, just happens to be humorless, but this is not fundamental to conjurers. may is a person that embodies seriousness and humour as naturally as ying and yang. it is reflecting of a shifting balance between clinging and letting go. something i've always admired. the ability to treat something as extremely important, but also being able to see something from the outside, seeing the process, understanding that the "i" of the moment is not the entire "i". this trait fits conjurers and transmuters.

may is possessed of nervousness that they keep under wraps, this type of interiority is also a hallmark of conjurers.

however, we cannot deny the fact that a lot of may's recent activity is centered around manipulation. both of me and of themself. so while the personality type fits conjurer better, there is still a lot of affinity through the nature of the nen type itself.

nen type conclusion

in the end i would say the compatibility between may and the nen types is as follows:

  • conjurer: 80%
  • manipulator: 65%
  • transmuter: 60%
  • enhancer: 15%
  • emitter: 0%

amusingly, the compatibility i list somewhat reflects the relationship conjurers have with other nen types.

ability

we will try to imagine what may's nen ability would be, assuming that may is a conjurer.

"whispers between the stars"

nen type: conjuration, manipulation, transmutation

the nen user can conjure a ring and two notebooks. the ring has two silver threads, each of them attached to one of the notebooks.

these threads are invisible to non-nen users, and always invisible to the target, even if they use gyo to try to observe the threads. any other nen user may observe the threads using gyo.

one notebook is conjured in the possession of the nen user and another is conjured in the possession of the target (or may be placed there at a later date). these two notebooks are, for most intents and purposes, the same notebook existing in two places at once.

gyo -- focusing aura into the eyes to observe the unseen.

level 1

the target blacks out 5-15 minutes every day and writes a short report of everything they've done that day and some of their thoughts. they have no awareness of doing this and immediately forget this having occurred.

anything written in the notebook by the target appears in the notebook of the nen user.

aspect:

  • the threads have a very faint silver glow, loosely laid on the floor, intersecting and ignoring walls.
  • even with gyo they might be easily missed if one is farther away than 10 yards.
  • the excessively winding nature of the threads obscures the location of the nen user.

severance:

  • if the thread is severed, the nen user is knocked unconscious for a few minutes.
  • this causes the notebook to burst into flames and disappear.

progression:

  • the ability progresses to the next stage once the nen user is capable of modelling with reasonable accuracy how the target behaves.
  • alternatively, the ability progresses if the target exhibits obsession over the nen user.

level 2

the target becomes fully aware of the notebook and considers it an important and voluntary part of their daily routine, though they do not understand the nature of the notebook and have trouble thinking of it as a nen ability.

the way the target regards the notebook depends on their personality:

  • they might view it as journaling to some secret friend.
  • they might view it as a self-improvement exercise.
  • they might view it as some sort of duty they must fulfill.

the nen user becomes capable of writing in the notebook, which can directly affect the target. the target will treat text added by the nen user as if it was something the target themself willingly wrote, unless the text is incongruent with what the target might normally write.

the nen user cannot write "i plan to kill myself tonight" without the target immediately questioning what is going on. however, the nen user can write more subtle things such as "i am beginning to dislike my organization." or "i wish to learn how to dance". these suggestions will take root much more easily.

the more negative or self-destructive the suggestion is, the lower the chance it will be successful, unless the individual is naturally inclined to such behaviors. the nen user has a lot of freedom in giving rise to inherent tendencies in the target.

additionally, the nen user becomes aware of the location of the notebook at all times.

aspect:

  • the threads starts shimmering, making them impossible to miss with gyo.
  • the threads become taut and give away the direction of the nen user.
  • the threads connect to the target's body directly instead of the notebook.
  • a secondary thread is conjured between the target and the notebook.

severance:

  • if the thread is dissolved by the target lashing against a suggestion, the nen user is knocked unconscious for a few minutes.
  • if the thread is severed, the target is knocked unconscious for a few minutes and forced in a state of zetsu for 24 hours.
  • this causes the notebook to burst into flames and disappear.

progression:

  • the ability progresses to the next stage once the nen user shares a vulnerable moment with the target.

level 3

the target will compulsively write their past and things that are important to them in the notebook.

the target is invariably obsessed with the nen user at this point and will write as if journaling to them. they will feel as if they have an inexorable link to the nen user, even though they will remain ignorant about the workings of the ability.

the nen user may alter existing pages but only if they replace them with something that is more compelling to the target. the target will experience alterations as a loss of relationship to previous memories or traits. they will feel as if those things belonged to a different person.

for each alteration, the target will learn something of commensurate importance about the nen user. the information learned can be detrimental to the nen user, but will never reveal the nature of the ability.

the information learned can deepen the target's obsession, or cause them to recoil, depending on the personalities of the user and the target, and their relationship.

aspect:

  • the threads coil around the target's neck and start fusing.
  • attempting to sever them causes intense pain to the target.

severance:

  • if the thread is severed, the target is knocked unconscious for a few minutes and forced in a state of zetsu for 24 hours.
  • the target wakes up grief stricken. for 24 hours they will actively seek the nen user to have the threads be re-attached. they will not be fully cognizant of what they are doing during this period.
  • the notebook combusts only after 24 hours have passed without re-attachment.

progression:

  • the ability can progress to the next stage once the nen user explains their ability in full to the target.
  • at this point the target may be released from the ability with no penalty if the target chooses to do so after the explanation.
  • if the target agrees to continue, they progress to the next level once they are familiar enough with the nen user to model their wishes independently.

level 4

the target writes in the notebook of their own initiative to change themself to be more useful to the nen user.

the nen user may write about themself in their own notebook. this has no special effect other than making the target aware of the nen user's way of being and their wishes. at this stage anything written in the notebook by the nen user is something that the target becomes aware of even without actively reading the notebook. however this has a latency of a few hours.

the nen user becomes intimately aware of the target's thoughts and feelings through the notebook, again with a latency. rather than receiving a "delayed stream" this feels more like becoming aware of the overall emotional and cognitive trajectory of the target in the past few hours.

aspect:

  • the threads are fused to the target's neck.
  • the threads inexplicably go upwards into the sky, rather than towards the nen user.
  • the notebook gains the appearance of a light blue tome with golden trim.

severance:

  • the threads can no longer be severed.

additional properties

  • the nen user may never have more than 10 active links.
  • all links of level 1 and 2 will quickly fade if the nen user does not maintain an active and strong interest in the target and vice versa.
  • once a target reaches level 3, the target's obsession on its own will indefinitely maintain the link.
  • if the nen user dies, a target with a level 4 link can choose to become the nen user. this is not reincarnation, but rather independent recreation of the original person. the process of this recreation is entirely non-supernatural and psychological, but supercharged by the intimate connection the level 4 link afforded. the new nen user will inherit all active links.
  • if a target dies and they have a level 4 link, their notebook persists a conjured item even after the thread dissipates. the notebook will be inhabited by post-mortem nen and the nen user will gain the ability to use one of the target's nen abilities (if they had one), provided the notebook is kept open and in hand. using the notebook in this way prevents the nen user from manipulating 5 of the threads.
  • if a target dies and they have a level 1-3 link, their notebook becomes just a regular object.

combat

  • the threads can become akin to razor wires rather than textile, but only in proximity to the nen user and only when aura is channeled down the conjured thread. the increased tensile strength and cutting power is an act of transmutation rather than alteration of the conjured threads themselves, the threads are merely conductors for transmuted aura.
  • the threads can be pinned to objects using conjured nails, which can be made invisible to those not actively using gyo.
  • this allows complex traps to be set up ahead of time, or for threads to be moved via the nails in the middle of combat, slicing an opponent apart or restraining them.
  • a level 4 thread cannot be severed, but if damaged will temporarily lose its ability to conduct aura, making a harmless albeit indestructible soft textile.
